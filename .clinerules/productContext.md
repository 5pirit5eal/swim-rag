# Product Context: Swim Training Plan Generator

## Core Purpose

The project is a web application designed for generating, recommending, and sharing training plans tailored for swimmers, triathletes, and trainers. Its primary goal is to simplify and enhance the process of creating and accessing effective training regimes.

## Problems Solved

- **Simplifies Training Plan Creation:** Addresses the complexity and time-consuming nature of designing suitable training plans from scratch.
- **Facilitates Sharing & Discovery:** Enables users to share their successful plans and discover plans created by others or scraped from the web, fostering a community and knowledge base.
- **Personalization (Future):** Aims to provide personalized training plans based on user input, history, and feedback, moving beyond generic templates.

## User Experience Goals

- **Ease of Use:** The application should be intuitive, allowing users to easily generate, view, export, and (in later versions) donate or share training plans.
- **Accessibility:** Plans should be exportable in common formats (initially PDF, later Excel/ODT) for offline use and printing.
- **Flexibility:** Users should be able to generate plans based on simple free-form text input or more detailed configuration menus.
- **Transparency (V1):** For the initial prototype, requests are anonymous, and user data donation is optional and handled with encryption.
- **Community & Collaboration (Future):** Later versions will introduce user accounts, history, sharing via URL, and a community board for popular plans.

## Target Users

- Swimmers (all levels)
- Triathletes
- Trainers and Coaches
